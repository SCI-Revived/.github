# SCI Revived Server Rules
The following are a series of rules pertaining to specific aspects of the SCI Revived Server. Please read each category carefully.

## General Rules
1. Do not attempt to propkill, propfly, or propblock. These are generally referred to as prop abuse.
2. Do not spam in chat or on the mic.
3. Do not use cheats or exploits.
4. Do not abuse addons for unintended behaviors that may impact the experience of other players (Ex. E2 Wallhacks, Nocolliding into bases, etc.)
5. Be respectful to players and staff.
6. Do not attempt to lag or crash the server.
7. Do not derail other people's trains.

## PVP Rules
These rules pertain to all PVP related matters. NOTE: This server uses Simple Prop Damage.

1. Do not abuse build/pvp mode to ambush players or to escape combat.
2. Do not spam Gbombs.
3. Do not spawnkill.
4. Do not set spawn inside bases without the owner's permission.
5. All Bases must have an entrance either secured with a keypad door or open.
6. Do not make indestructible bases.
